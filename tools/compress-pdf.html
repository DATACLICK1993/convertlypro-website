🗜️ Compress PDF

Reduce PDF file size by re-optimizing the internal structure. Browser-based with pdf-lib — your files stay private.

ℹ️ How This Works: pdf-lib re-saves the PDF with object streams enabled, removing duplicate objects and optimizing structure. Best results on PDFs with lots of metadata or form data. For heavy image compression, a server-side tool achieves higher reduction.

What Gets Compressed

✅ Works Well

PDFs with lots of metadata, duplicate objects, form data, and unoptimized structure.

⚠️ Limited Effect

PDFs that are already optimized, or PDFs where size comes from embedded images.

How much will it compress?+
Varies widely — from 5% to 40%+ depending on the PDF's internal structure. Results shown after processing.
Why not more compression?+
Heavy image resampling requires server-side processing. Our browser tool optimizes what it can without a server.
