Does it upload my PDF anywhere?+
No — PDF.js renders your PDF entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your file never leaves your device.
How many pages can it convert?+
Any number of pages. Each page becomes a separate JPG. All pages download together as a single ZIP file.
Why does it take longer for big PDFs?+
Each page is rendered separately by your browser. A 50-page PDF at 300 DPI may take 30-60 seconds — this is normal.
Will scanned PDFs work?+
Yes — scanned PDFs convert perfectly since each page is already an image. DPI setting affects the output resolution.
