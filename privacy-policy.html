Data We Collect

For image conversion tools that run in your browser: no data is collected. Files never leave your device.

For PDF and document tools requiring server processing: files are temporarily uploaded using encrypted HTTPS connections and automatically deleted within 1 hour.

Google Analytics & Advertising

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (all data is anonymized — no personal information is collected). We display ads through Google AdSense. These services use cookies to serve ads and may collect anonymous usage data.

Google AdSense & DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third-party advertising vendor, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads to our users based on their visits to this site and other websites on the internet. The DART cookie enables Google to display ads relevant to your interests.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Our third-party ad partners (including Google) may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to:

  • Measure ad performance and analytics
  • Show personalized ads based on your browsing history
  • Frequency capping (limiting how often you see the same ad)
  • Reporting and fraud detection

ConvertlyPro has no access or control over these third-party cookies. You can opt out of personalized advertising at AboutAds.info or via Network Advertising Initiative.
